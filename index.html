Contrast Ratio Guide

  • 1:1 – No contrast, unreadable
  • 2:1 – Barely visible, difficult for most users
  • 3:1 – Low contrast, hard for some users to read
  • 4:1 – Minimum for large text
  • 4.5:1 – Minimum for regular text (WCAG recommended)
  • 5:1 – Slightly better readability
  • 6:1 – Good contrast for most users
  • 7:1 – Recommended for optimal readability
  • 8:1 – High contrast, easily readable by everyone
  • 9:1 – Very high contrast, clear and crisp
  • 10:1 – Excellent for readability
  • 15:1 – Super easy to read
  • 20:1 – Extremely high contrast, very legible
  • 21:1 – Maximum contrast, ideal for accessibility

Higher ratios improve accessibility, with 7:1 and above being great for readability.
